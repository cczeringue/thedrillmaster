Co-Creator

Caleb Zeringue

C

Caleb is a stand-up comic, writer, and co-creator of Luigi the Musical. As a stand-up, he's a NYC Queer Comedy Festival finalist, featured at Edinburgh Fringe, LGBTLolFest, Vice City Comedy Festival and featured for national headliners including Jenny Zigrino and Lady Bushra — who STILL won't talk to him. He grew up across 16 states (his dad insists he's not bipolar, why do you ask?!). His comedy is unapologetically queer and southern, with a suspicious amount of emotional vulnerability and "bless your heart" energy.

He and Jenny Zigrino (Dropout.TV, Sirius XM) are part of the writing team of a new theatrical production, The Drillmaster, with a first reading set for May 3rd at The Elysian.
